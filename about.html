Our Story

Dedicated to creating the finest functional treats for the dogs we love

Born from Love, Perfected by Science

PureTreats was founded in 2023 by a team of passionate pet parents and veterinary nutritionists who believed that our four-legged family members deserved better than generic treats filled with artificial ingredients and empty calories.

Our journey began when our founder's senior Golden Retriever, Max, started experiencing joint issues. Frustrated by the lack of truly effective, natural solutions, we set out to create treats that would not only taste amazing but also provide real, functional benefits.

Today, every PureTreats product is the result of extensive research, careful sourcing of premium ingredients, and rigorous testing to ensure safety and efficacy. We believe that functional nutrition for dogs should be as sophisticated and trustworthy as it is for humans.
