What makes PureTreats different from regular dog treats?

+

PureTreats are specifically formulated functional treats designed to support specific aspects of your dog's health. Unlike regular treats that focus primarily on taste, our treats contain clinically researched ingredients that provide targeted benefits like joint support, stress relief, or digestive health. Each treat is carefully crafted by veterinary nutritionists to deliver both great taste and measurable health benefits.

Which PureTreat product is right for my dog?

+

The right product depends on your dog's specific needs:

  • Daily Wellness: Perfect for overall health maintenance and immune support
  • Joint Support: Ideal for active dogs, seniors, or breeds prone to joint issues
  • Calming Blend: Great for anxious dogs or stressful situations like travel or fireworks
  • Skin & Coat: Helps dogs with skin issues or those needing a shinier coat
  • Digestive Health: Perfect for dogs with sensitive stomachs or digestive issues

When in doubt, consult with your veterinarian about which functional benefits would be most appropriate for your dog.

Can I give my dog multiple PureTreat products?

+

Yes, many of our products can be safely combined. However, we recommend starting with one product to assess your dog's response, then gradually introducing additional products if needed. Always follow the feeding guidelines on each package and consult with your veterinarian before combining multiple functional supplements, especially if your dog is on medication.

What ingredients do you use in PureTreats?

+

We use only premium, natural ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers. Our treats contain:

  • High-quality proteins (chicken, salmon, or lamb)
  • Functional ingredients like glucosamine, probiotics, omega fatty acids
  • Natural flavors and preservatives
  • Superfoods like sweet potato, blueberries, and spinach

We never use artificial colors, flavors, or by-products. Full ingredient lists are available on each product page.

Are your treats made in the USA?

+

Yes, all PureTreats are manufactured in FDA-registered facilities in the United States. We maintain strict quality control standards and follow Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) to ensure the highest quality and safety standards.

Do you test for contaminants and heavy metals?

+

Absolutely. Every batch is tested by third-party laboratories for contaminants, heavy metals, bacteria, and other potential safety concerns. We maintain certificates of analysis for all our products and ingredients.

Are PureTreats safe for puppies and senior dogs?

+

Most of our treats are formulated for adult dogs (12+ months). However, we do have specific formulations for different life stages:

  • Puppy formulations: Available for joint development and general wellness
  • Senior formulations: Enhanced with ingredients to support aging dogs
  • Adult maintenance: Our standard formulations for healthy adult dogs

Always check the product label for age recommendations and consult your veterinarian before giving functional treats to puppies under 12 months or dogs with health conditions.

Can dogs with allergies eat PureTreats?

+

We offer several limited-ingredient formulations for dogs with food sensitivities. Common allergens are clearly labeled on all our products. If your dog has known allergies, carefully review the ingredient list and consult with your veterinarian. We also offer grain-free and single-protein options for dogs with specific dietary restrictions.

How long before I see results from functional treats?

+

Results vary depending on the specific function and your dog's individual response:

  • Calming effects: May be noticed within 30-60 minutes
  • Digestive support: Often seen within 1-2 weeks of consistent use
  • Skin & coat improvements: Typically visible after 4-6 weeks
  • Joint support: May take 6-8 weeks of consistent use to see full benefits

Consistency is key - functional ingredients work best when given regularly as directed.

How many treats should I give my dog per day?

+

Feeding amounts depend on your dog's weight and the specific product:

  • Small dogs (under 25 lbs): 1-2 treats per day
  • Medium dogs (25-50 lbs): 2-3 treats per day
  • Large dogs (50+ lbs): 3-4 treats per day

Always follow the specific feeding guidelines on each product package. Treats should not exceed 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake.

Can I use PureTreats as training rewards?

+

Yes! Our treats are perfect for training sessions. They're made in convenient sizes and most dogs find them highly palatable. For training, you may want to break larger treats into smaller pieces to avoid overfeeding while still maintaining the functional benefits. Just be sure to account for training treats in your dog's daily feeding guidelines.

Should I give treats with meals or separately?

+

This depends on the specific functional ingredient and your dog's preferences:

  • Digestive support treats: Best given with meals
  • Calming treats: Give 30-60 minutes before stressful events
  • Joint support treats: Can be given with or between meals
  • General wellness: Flexible timing, but consistency is important

Check each product's specific recommendations for optimal timing and effectiveness.
